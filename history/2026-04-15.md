## Deals for April 15, 2026

The current market environment, with the VIX at a slightly elevated 18.17, presents a favorable backdrop for option sellers. This level of volatility is not indicative of panic but suggests enough uncertainty to inflate option premiums, creating attractive risk/reward opportunities for income-focused strategies. Our focus will be on high-probability, defined-risk trades on high-quality large-cap technology stocks that are showing signs of either short-term consolidation or are approaching key technical support levels.
