## Deals for May 08, 2026

With the VIX at a moderate 17.19, the market is not exhibiting extreme fear, suggesting that option premiums are not excessively inflated but still offer attractive income opportunities. This environment is conducive to high-probability strategies like selling credit spreads and covered calls, which benefit from time decay (theta) and do not require significant market volatility. The following deals are conditional, designed to be executed if specific price levels or conditions are met, allowing for disciplined entry into positions with a favorable risk/reward profile.
