## Deals for July 10, 2026

This makes selling premium slightly less lucrative than in high-VIX scenarios, but it also points to more predictable, range-bound price action. Consequently, our focus shifts to high-probability, defined-risk strategies. Credit spreads and iron condors are particularly well-suited, as they allow us to collect premium with a statistical edge while strictly managing potential losses. The following recommendations are conditional, designed to be executed if the underlying asset reaches specific technical or psychological price levels that improve the trade's probability of success.
